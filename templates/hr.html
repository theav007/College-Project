Problem Statement

There is a company named Best Wishes Finance that deals in all loans. They have to promot there 100 employee's,so the HR decide who is right candidate to be promoted there are more then 50k employees in the company so it will take lot time to decide right candidate manually so Machine Learning Team develop a model which is more accurate and fast to decide right candidate with some information.

Identify the factors that are eligible for promotion.

There are 11 columns in our data set. Of them is_promoted is the response variable and rest all are the variables /factors that decide the promotion of the employee or not.
department : Person should have only one Department.

Sales & Marketing
Operations
Technology
Analytics

Legal
education : Education Level's.

Master's & above
Bachelor's
Below Secondary
gender : Male or Female.
no_of_trainings: Number of other trainings completed in previous year on soft skills, technical skills etc. 0 to 10
age : Employee Age.
previous_year_rating : Employee Rating for the previous year.1 to 5
length_of_service : Length of service in years.
KPIs_met >80% : If Percent of KPIs(Key performance Indicators) >80% then 1 else 0
awards_won? : If awards won during previous year then 1 else 0
avg_training_score : Average score in current training evaluations40 to 99
is_promoted : If the employee is eligible for promotion it’s yes represented by Y else it’s no represented by N.
